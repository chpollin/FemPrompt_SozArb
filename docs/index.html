About This Project

The Social AI Literature Vault is an automated research pipeline that analyzes academic literature on bias, fairness, and intersectionality in AI systems. Using multi-model AI synthesis, we extract key concepts and relationships from research papers to build a comprehensive knowledge graph.

This site presents the results of our analysis, making it easy to explore research findings, discover conceptual relationships, and identify key themes in the field.
