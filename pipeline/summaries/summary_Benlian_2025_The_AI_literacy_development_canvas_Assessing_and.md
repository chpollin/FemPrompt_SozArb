## Overview

This research addresses a fundamental disconnect—organizations invest in AI technology while neglecting workforce preparedness. The study establishes that inadequate AI understanding, not technology limitations, drives organizational risk and missed value creation. The authors argue that AI literacy—encompassing conceptual understanding, ethical reasoning, and practical competencies—must be systematically assessed and developed across all organizational levels.
