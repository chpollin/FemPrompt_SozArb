# Algorithmic Transparency and Interpretability

## Definition

The requirement for child welfare agencies and practitioners to understand the limitations, decision boundaries, and operational logic of algorithmic tools, supported by clear documentation, external ethical review, and feedback mechanisms for continuous improvement.
