# Intersectional Algorithmic Discrimination

## Definition

The compounded negative effects of algorithmic bias on individuals with overlapping marginalized identities (race, gender, socioeconomic status), resulting in systematic disadvantage across critical domains like welfare, criminal justice, and healthcare.
